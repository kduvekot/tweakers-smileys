=== CRITICAL INSIGHT ===

The GIF achieves the "shake" animation by:
1. Moving the ENTIRE face content (eyes + mouth + outline) horizontally
2. X offset ranges from 0 (center) to 4 pixels (maximum right)
3. The eyes and mouth DO NOT move independently
4. They are PART OF THE SAME CONTENT BLOB that shifts together

This means:
- Eyes stay in fixed positions relative to the face
- Mouth stays in fixed position relative to the face
- NO perspective distortion
- NO independent eye/mouth movement
- The animation is PURE TRANSLATION (no rotation, no scaling, no morphing)
