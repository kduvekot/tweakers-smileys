Summary of Worship Animation

All three components animate together in coordination:

  • Face: Moves down 8px and squishes vertically to 50% (bowing motion)
  • Both Hands: Move down 11px and squish to 40% height (hands going down)
  • Left Hand: Additionally skews 10deg right (hand angle)
  • Right Hand: Additionally skews 10deg left (mirror of left hand)

This creates a synchronized "bowing/worshipping" motion where everything goes down together and comes back up together.
